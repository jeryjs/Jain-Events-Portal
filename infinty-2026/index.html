About Faculty of Engineering & Technology

With a decade of experience, the Faculty of Engineering & Technology at JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) (JU-FET) is the prominent engineering institution in Bengaluru. B.Tech., M.Tech., and specialised programmes are available at JU-FET. JU-FET is committed to delivering an education of the highest calibre. By utilising the practical and forward-thinking approach, in addition to the superior teaching and research facilities, students will receive a unique and high-caliber academic experience that is centred on skill and knowledge development. This will facilitate a seamless transition from academia to the business world.
