🆔 ABC ID Registration
Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) ID is a unique identification for storing and managing your academic credits. This is required for credit transfer and academic mobility as per NEP 2020.
💡 About ABC ID
What is ABC ID?
  • A digital repository to store your academic credits
  • Enables seamless credit transfer between institutions
  • Required for degree verification and future admissions
  • One-time registration valid throughout your academic journey
Required Information: Aadhar Card, Email ID, Mobile Number, and Academic Details
